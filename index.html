About Me

I'm an aspiring Full Stack Developer with 17 years experience in the healthcare industry. Proven ability to tackle any challenges to meet and achieve the best possible outcome. Proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, mySQL, and Sharepoint. Looking to apply my knowledge of web development in a like-minded environment with a dynamic team to ensure technical feasibility while maximizing users experience.

The technology that I'm interested in exploring more is social media, start-ups, film & media, government and homeland securities. One day, I hope to be able to work on a project that can help first responders find people or pets easily during crises.

My current skillset includes HTML5 & CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Node.js, Express, Heroku deployment, GitHub, Firebase, MySQL and Sequelize, Sharepoint, and I'm still learning. I started coding about three years ago when I took my first computer science class, I fell in love with it. I stay up to date with current technologies by reading blogs and attending meet-ups across the Bay Area. I love taking the time to perfect projects and bring life to my (and hopefully your) ideas! I'm an easy-going, kind, down to earth, fun person to be around, but also a very disciplined person with drive and passion to follow through and complete projects with perfection.

In my free time I like to explore the bay area, try new restaurants, meet new people, hang with my dog Rocky, read a bit, and watch movies.
